Who Is Buzzy?


Buzzy is an animatronic at the Wonders of Life pavilion at Walt Disney World's EPCOT Center, and is part of the show/attraction Cranium Command. He debuted on October 19th, 1989, and served until January 1st, 2007, when his attraction was shut down permanently. He sat dormant in his attraction until approximately December 21st, 2018, when he was reported missing! If you have any knowledge as to his current whereabouts, please contact Walt Disney Imagineering!

What was the Wonders of Life Pavilion?


The Wonders of Life Pavilion was the final pavilion opened at EPCOT Center, now known simply as Epcot, and was themed to Healthy Living and the Human Body. It only contained two attractions, Body Wars, and the previously mentioned Cranium Command. It did contain a few shows and small exhibits based around the themes of Healthy Living, including Goofy About Health, a multimedia show hosted by Goofy and using clips from his cartoons that promoted healthy living, Sensory Funhouse, an interactive playground, and the all-too-popular The Making of Me, a short movie about birth and life starring Martin Short.
